Movie : FLOW

Flow, a mesmerizing and visually stunning animated short film, was released in 2023. It was directed by the talented Gitanjali Rao and produced by the renowned Walt Disney Animation Studios.

Flow, released in 2023 and directed by Gitanjali Rao and produced by Walt Disney Animation Studios, tells a heartwarming story about a young girl named Maya. Afraid of the water after a traumatic experience, Maya struggles to connect with her father, a fisherman, and his love of the sea. Through music and her grandmother's guidance, Maya rediscovers her connection to the water, learns to cope with her fear, and ultimately finds healing and reconciliation with her family.

STUDIO GHIBLI'S My Neighbour Totoro

Two young sisters, Satsuki and Mei, move to the countryside with their father to be closer to their hospitalized mother. While exploring their new surroundings, Mei encounters a small, furry forest spirit, eventually leading her to the larger, cuddly creature known as Totoro. Satsuki soon meets Totoro as well, and the sisters embark on magical adventures with the benevolent forest spirit and his catbus companion. Totoro helps them cope with their mother's illness and the anxieties of their new life, offering comfort, wonder, and a touch of magic during a challenging time. The film celebrates the power of imagination, the beauty of nature, and the importance of family.
